Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

1. What formats can I download my business card in?

You can download your business card in PNG.

2. Can I customize the design of the business card?

You can upload your logo or any other graphics you want to include. With premium subscription, our Business Card Generator offers a wide range of customizable templates, color schemes, and fonts to match your brand identity.

3. Is there a limit to the number of cards I can generate?

You can get 1 free business card. With premium subscription, you can generate and download as many business cards as you need!

4. Can I save my design for future edits?

Yes, you can save your design and come back anytime to make edits or modifications. Just ensure you're logged in so that your designs are saved to your profile.

5. What's the difference between the free and premium versions?

The free version allows users to create and preview business card designs, while the premium version provides high-resolution downloads, access to premium templates, and priority customer support.

6. How do I get in touch for support or feedback?

You can reach out to us via the 'About Us' section on our website, or directly email us at support@businesscardgenerator.com. We value your feedback and will get back to you as soon as possible!
